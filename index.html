A word from our priest

A warm welcome

“It is a joy to welcome you. Come and join us.”

We are a discerning, inclusive faith community, rich in ethnicities, cultures and traditions — truly blessed with St Paul’s variety of gifts, and flavoured with humankind’s sprinkling of weaknesses.

If you’re new, simply introduce yourself to one of our welcomers at the next Mass. We’d love to meet you.

Fr. Anthony ChiatuParish Priest
